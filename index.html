THE TITANS

During the Mesozoic Era, over 250 million years ago, Earth was dominated by a group of reptiles known as dinosaurs. From colossal claws to tiny teeth, these creatures ran amok throughout the Triassic, Jurassic, and Cretaceous periods. After the asteroid caused their extinction approximately 66 million years ago, scientific research has let us slowly rediscover their world.
